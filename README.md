# Quick Stash

A Fabric mod for Minecraft 1.21.11 that lets you sort your inventory into a chest (or any other storage container) with a single keypress — and lock specific items so they never get swept away, Terraria-style.

## Features

- **Quick sort** — press `G` while a container is open to send your inventory items straight into it.
- **Quick retrieve** — hold `Shift` and press `G` to pull everything back out of the container into your inventory.
- **Item locking** — hover over an item and press `L` to lock it. Locked items are skipped by sorting, no matter how many slots you move them through.
- **Visual indicator** — locked slots are highlighted so you always know what's protected.
- Works with chests, trapped chests, ender chests, barrels, shulker boxes, hoppers, dispensers, and droppers.

## Controls

| Action | Key |
|---|---|
| Sort inventory into container | `G` |
| Sort container into inventory | `Shift` + `G` |
| Lock / unlock item | `L` |

Keybindings can be remapped in **Options > Controls**.

## Why locking?

Sorting mods are great until they accidentally stash your favorite sword in a chest full of cobblestone. Quick Stash solves this the same way Terraria does: lock the items you care about, and they'll stay exactly where you put them no matter how many times you hit the sort key.

## Installation

1. Install [Fabric Loader](https://fabricmc.net/use/) for Minecraft 1.21.11.
2. Install [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api).
3. Drop the Quick Stash `.jar` into your `mods` folder.
